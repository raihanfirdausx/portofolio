Car Price Prediction

When we want to buy or sell a car, sometimes we have no idea how to decide the price. One easy way is to find out in some automobile marketplaces, but finding an exact and precise number is quite hard. In this project, I'm going to make a machine learning that will allow us to know the price of a car based on several factors like engine-size, fuel-eficiency, and etc. Hopefully, this machine learning can help anyone who want to find out how much the car's price they want to buy/sell should be.

    • Economic Crisis Prediction

    Economic condition is an important consideration in business, and one fundamental issue about it is economic crisis. If we could predict whether there will be a crisis or not, we can make better decisions. Supposed we already know that an economic crisis will occur, then we can take any suitable decisions to face it; perhaps we have to postpone a project, hold a launching of new product, sell unproductive assets to gain more cash, and any other decisions that could save our company from many potential loss. Hence, in this project I make a machine learning that will be able to predict whether a crisis will occur or not based on several economic variables.

    Football Talent Recommender

    Supposed you are a football talent scout agent and you are assigned to find a new player for a club. It has been quite difficult to define the player that we want. Instead of saying "Find me a striker who can run up to 70 km/h and kick the ball as hard as 1500 Pounds force", we are more likely to say "Find me a striker like Lionel Messi". It is much easier to define what kind of player that we want by mentioning other player's name who is likely similar rather than literally define it by some numbers of measurement. Hence, as a football lover and data scientist, I make a Recommender System that will allow us to find a very similar talent based on one single name we input as a model.
